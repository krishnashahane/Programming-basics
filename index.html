                  About SymbiVibe

                  SymbiVibe was founded by Krishna — a First Year Cybersecurity student at Symbiosis Skills and Professional University, Pune (CSIT). Frustrated by how fragmented campus communication felt — scattered across WhatsApp groups, notice boards, and word-of-mouth — Krishna set out to build something better: a real-time social pulse for every corner of Symbiosis.

                  As Founder & CEO of SymbiVibe, Krishna envisioned a platform where every student could vibe together across the Amphi, Nescafe Lounge, Canteen, Library, and beyond — all in one place, live and instantaneous. SymbiVibe is mobile-first and works beautifully on any device.

                  SymbiVibe is growing into the definitive digital heartbeat of Symbiosis campus life — real-time chats, live event feeds, queue trackers, and more. Curated for students, by a student who lives it every single day.
